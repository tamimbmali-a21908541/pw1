About Me

My motivations for choosing the course were mainly driven by my passion for technology and my desire to learn more about computers and their applications. I was particularly interested in programming and coding, and I wanted to pursue a career in the tech industry. On the course, I have most enjoyed learning about coding in different programming languages, creating and building software programs and applications, and understanding computer hardware and software components. I also appreciate learning about the latest trends and advancements in the tech industry.

Regarding my qualities, I believe I have strong problem-solving skills, am detail-oriented and analytical, and am good at working independently as well as in a team. I am also willing to learn and adapt to new technologies.

When I finish the course, my expectations are to pursue a career in software development or computer engineering, work for a tech company and contribute to the development of new and innovative products, or start my own tech-related business. In my free time, I enjoy coding and programming personal projects, playing video games and analyzing game mechanics, reading about technology news and advancements, and attending tech-related events and workshops.
